# Inventory App - User Guide & Troubleshooting

## Barcode Scanning

### Problem: Barcode scanner won't read codes
**Symptoms:** App opens camera but doesn't recognize barcodes
**Common Causes:** Camera focus issues, poor lighting, damaged barcode

**Solution Steps:**
1. Clean your camera lens with a soft cloth
2. Ensure adequate lighting - avoid shadows on the barcode
3. Hold phone 6-8 inches from barcode
4. Keep phone steady and let autofocus work
5. If barcode is damaged, try manual entry

### Problem: Wrong product scanned
**Symptoms:** Scanned barcode shows different product
**Common Causes:** Similar barcode numbers, database mismatch

**Solution Steps:**
1. Verify the barcode number matches the product label
2. Try scanning from the product's original packaging
3. Use the search function to find product manually
4. Report the mismatch to inventory management

## Stock Management

### Problem: Stock count doesn't match physical inventory
**Symptoms:** App shows different quantity than actual count

**Solution Steps:**
1. Perform a recount to verify physical quantity
2. Check if any recent transfers are pending sync
3. Review transaction history for that item
4. Submit a stock adjustment request through the app

### Problem: Unable to transfer stock between locations
**Symptoms:** Transfer button is grayed out or shows error

**Solution Steps:**
1. Verify you have permission for both locations
2. Check that destination location has available capacity
3. Ensure item is not locked for audit
4. Confirm network connectivity
5. Contact supervisor if restrictions persist
